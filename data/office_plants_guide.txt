Snake plants require minimal watering and thrive in low-light office environments with minimal maintenance.
Pothos vines grow quickly and help purify air while adding natural green aesthetics to workspace areas.
Desk-sized succulents provide stress relief and require watering only once every two weeks.
Large floor plants like fiddle leaf figs create natural room dividers and improve office ambiance.
Spider plants are nearly indestructible and produce baby plants that can be shared with colleagues.
Peace lilies bloom periodically and indicate watering needs through their drooping leaves signal system.
Rubber plants have glossy leaves that add visual interest and grow well in standard office lighting.
Air plants require no soil and can be mounted on walls or placed in decorative containers.
Plant care schedules help maintain office greenery and distribute watering responsibilities among team members.
